Ceremony

The Sage Chapel ceremony rehearsal is Friday, May 12th at 5:00pm. Once everyone's fully rehearsed, we will carpool to the Argos Inn for dinner and drinks from 6:30pm to 10pm. We have reserved some parking spots at the Inn's lot.

Dinner Venue

The historic Argos Inn is housed in an impeccably renovated historic mansion. It's bar is widely considered to produce the finest libations in all of Ithaca. They also have Genessee Tallboys for those looking for more humble potation.

Dinner Cuisine

In addition to cocktails and conversation, there will be a pig roast for dinner, provided by Luna Inspired Street Food. Please let us know of any dietary restrictions.

Photography

Instead of hiring a photographer, we decided to crowdsource the responsibility of capturing the evening. We've created a shared Google Photos album, and are hoping that guests will take photos throughout the night and upload them to the album.
